Connection Instructions

1

Log in to your domain registrar (e.g., GoDaddy, Namecheap).

2

Find your domain's DNS (Domain Name System) settings.

3

Create or update the following CNAME record and AAA record:

Type: A Name: @ Value: {{ domain.domain }}

Type: CNAME Name: www Value: {{ domain.domain }}

4

Save your changes. DNS updates can take up to 24 hours to propagate.
