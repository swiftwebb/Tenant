Connect Your Domain

Follow the steps below to point your domain to your new SiteCraft website.

public

Connection Instructions

1

Log in to your domain registrar (e.g., GoDaddy, Namecheap).

2

Find your domain's DNS (Domain Name System) settings.

3

Create or update the following DNS records:

Type: A Name: @ Value: 192.0.2.1

Type: CNAME Name: www Value: yourdomain.com

4

Save your changes. DNS updates can take up to 24 hours to propagate.
