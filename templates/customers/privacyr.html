Introduction

Welcome to Baxting! This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our website-building platform and services. At Baxting, we are committed to safeguarding your data and ensuring your privacy is respected at all times.

database

Information We Collect

We collect information from you in the following ways:

  • person_add
    Information you provide directly: When you create an account, publish a website, purchase hosting, or contact support. This may include your name, email address, business information, and payment details.
  • analytics
    Information collected automatically: We collect data about your interactions with Baxting, such as IP address, browser type, device information, pages visited, and session activity to help optimize performance and security.
  • cookie
    Cookies and similar technologies: Cookies allow us to remember your settings, improve platform functionality, and analyze traffic. You may disable cookies anytime through your browser settings, though some features may not operate correctly.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • web
    Provide and maintain the Baxting platform including website creation, hosting, templates, and builder tools.
  • auto_awesome
    Improve and personalize user experience through platform updates, analytics, and feature enhancements.
  • mail
    Send service-related communication including account updates, system notifications, security alerts, and billing information.
  • verified_user
    Enhance security by preventing fraud, abuse, or unauthorized access to your sites or account.

Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your data. However, information may be shared in limited circumstances:

  • engineering
    Service providers: Baxting works with hosting, analytics, payment, and security partners. These providers are required to protect your information and use it only to support our platform.
  • gavel
    Legal obligations: We may disclose information if legally required or in response to valid regulatory or investigative requests.
  • business
    Business transfers: If Baxting undergoes a merger, acquisition, restructuring, or asset sale, user data may transfer to the acquiring party as part of continuity of service.

We employ industry-standard security practices, encryption, and access controls to protect your information. While we take every reasonable measure to secure data, no online transmission or storage method is 100% guaranteed.
